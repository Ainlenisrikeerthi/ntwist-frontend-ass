Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our application.

What is this application?

This is a frontend application built for the NTWIST. It demonstrates HTML, CSS, and JavaScript skills through three different pages: a contact form, a sortable list, and this FAQ section.

How do I use the contact form?

Fill in your name, email, and message in the form fields. All fields are required. Once you've completed the form, click the "Submit" button. A confirmation message will appear, and the form will be cleared.

How does the list sorting work?

The names are sorted alphabetically using JavaScript. When you click the "Sort Alphabetically" button, our script rearranges the list items in alphabetical order without reloading the page.

Is this website responsive?

Yes, this website is fully responsive and works well on all devices including desktops, tablets, and mobile phones. The layout will automatically adjust based on your screen size.

How can I provide feedback?

You can provide feedback by using the star rating system on the Contact page or by sending us a message through the contact form.
